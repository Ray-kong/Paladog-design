# Character Document — v1.0 (PD Final Version)

> Principle: You should immediately know what a character does just by seeing its name/appearance.
> No elemental attributes, distinguished only by attack type, all 20 units have unique roles.
> Implementation constraints: No attaching to enemies, no ground installations

---

## [C-01] Sprout — Protagonist

- **Name**: Sprout
- **Alias/Title**: Arca's last sprout, Little Guardian
- **Role**: Guardian and commander of the Last Garden. Hero unit.
- **Motivation**: Carrying on Arca's will to protect the garden and purify the blighted land. Gradually finding their own reasons beyond the mission.
- **Flaw**: Does not know what the world originally looked like. Naivety sometimes leads to poor judgment.
- **Background**: During the Withering, Arca produced several sprouts, but Sprout was the only one to survive. Arca has no strength left to produce new sprouts. **Sprout's death = defeat.**
- **Key Relationships**:
  - Arca: Parent and creator
  - Garden Guardians: Allies germinated from seeds
  - The Blighted: Enemies, but originally beings of the same world
- **Sample Dialogue**:
  - "What color was this land originally... I really want to see it with my own eyes."
  - "I can't run away. There's no one else but me."
  - "Were you once a flower too...? I'm sorry. But I have to stop you."

## Allied Units — 20 Types (Final Version)

### Ch1: Sprout Series (Around the Last Garden)

#### 1. Thorn — Basic Melee Dealer [R1]
- **Appearance**: A cactus-type spirit densely covered in sharp thorns. Short arms covered in spikes
- **Why?**: Thorns = stabbing. The simplest attack
- **Animation**: Rapidly thrusts its spiky arms. The way it busily stabs with its short arms is cute
- **Personality**: Impatient and aggressive. *"Hurry, hurry! I'll stab everything in front of me!"*

#### 2. Acorn — HP Tank [R10]
- **Appearance**: A plump spirit in acorn shell armor. Wooden shield
- **Why?**: Acorn = hard shell = endures without breaking
- **Animation**: Puffs up shell as shield. Bounces when hit. Cracks appear but regenerate
- **Personality**: Cowardly but never abandons position. *"I-I'm scared but... everyone's behind me!"*

#### 3. Dandy — Ranged Dealer [R20]
- **Appearance**: A spirit whose head is a cluster of dandelion seeds
- **Why?**: Seeds = ride the wind and fly far = natural ranged attacker
- **Animation**: Plucks a seed and blows it. Travels in an arc to hit enemies
- **Personality**: Relaxed and dreamy. *"Nice breeze~ They'll fly well today."*

#### 4. Snail — Slow Support [R40 Purification]
- **Appearance**: A snail spirit carrying a glowing shell on its back. Silver slime drips from its body
- **Why?**: Snail = synonym for slowness + slime. When it spits slime, enemies slow down — the living icon of slow
- **Animation**: Spits a glob of silver slime from its mouth as a ranged projectile. Hit enemies get covered in sticky slime, reducing movement/attack speed. Sparkling slime dripping effect
- **Personality**: Slow but steady. *"Slowly... slowly... if you rush, you lose~"*
- **Purification Boss**: Toxic Moss — acid moss that dissolves on contact -> After purification, becomes a guardian of slime that slows enemies

### Ch2: Forest Beast Series (Forest Beyond the Garden)

#### 5. Hedgie — AoE Dealer [R1]
- **Appearance**: A hedgehog with glowing spines. Can curl into a ball
- **Why?**: Curls into a spike ball -> rolling hits all enemies on contact = AoE textbook
- **Animation**: Rolls into a spike ball and charges through enemy lines, hitting all nearby. Gets dizzy when uncurling
- **Personality**: Easygoing and bold. *"All bunched up? Even better!"*

#### 6. Bee — Healer [R10]
- **Appearance**: A plump bee. Carries a honey pot around
- **Why?**: Honey = healing. The antibacterial/healing properties of honey have been common knowledge since ancient times
- **Animation**: Scoops honey from the pot and applies it to allies. Golden honey coating -> HP recovery
- **Personality**: Diligent and affectionate. *"Hold still, I'll put some honey on it! Bear with the stickiness~"*

#### 7. Bear — Taunt Tank [R20]
- **Appearance**: A large bear. Intimidating when standing on hind legs
- **Why?**: Bear growl = all eyes on it = taunt
- **Animation**: Stands on hind legs and roars. All enemies in range switch target to Bear
- **Personality**: Solid and dependable. *"...Come at me. I'll take it all."*

#### 8. Woody — Multi-Hit Dealer [R40 Purification]
- **Appearance**: A woodpecker with a hard beak. Red crest feathers
- **Why?**: Woodpecker = rapid "rat-tat-tat-tat" pecking = the perfect natural symbol of multi-hit
- **Animation**: Latches onto enemy and pecks with beak at high speed "rat-tat-tat-tat-tat!" Drum roll sound. Speed gradually increases
- **Personality**: Silently just works. *"(rat-tat-tat-tat-tat)" — no dialogue, only impact sounds*
- **Purification Boss**: Frenzied Raptor — a being that pecked and destroyed wildly -> After purification, becomes a warrior of precise multi-hit strikes

### Ch3: Ancient Tree Series (Deep Forest)

#### 9. Scorpion — DoT (Damage over Time) Dealer [R1]
- **Appearance**: A scorpion spirit with a glowing stinger. Tail raised threateningly
- **Why?**: Scorpion = venomous stinger. When stung, the poison spreads and hurts more over time = the natural symbol of DoT
- **Animation**: Swings tail to sting enemies. Purple poison effect spreads on stung enemies dealing damage over time. Can sting multiple enemies to stack poison
- **Personality**: Calm and cold. *"One sting is enough. The poison does the rest."*

#### 10. Oakguard — Knockback Tank [R10]
- **Appearance**: A massive oak trunk golem. Moss and mushrooms growing on it
- **Why?**: When a giant tree golem swings its arms, everything in front gets pushed back = nature's bulldozer
- **Animation**: Raises massive tree arm and sweeps. Enemies get pushed back like waves
- **Personality**: Extremely slow and silent. *"...Will push." (thud)*

#### 11. Bloom — Buffer [R20]
- **Appearance**: A palm-sized fairy with petal wings
- **Why?**: Pollen = life's vitality. When pollen touches allies, Arca's blessing dwells within and strengthens them
- **Animation**: Flutters wings and scatters golden pollen. Allies glow and stats increase. Small flowers bloom above their heads
- **Personality**: Cheerful and chatty. *"Blessings~! Can everyone feel yourselves getting stronger?"*

#### 12. Shroom — Summoner Support [R40 Purification]
- **Appearance**: A chubby mushroom spirit. Spores rise from the cap
- **Why?**: When spores spread, new mushrooms grow = the most natural form of summoning
- **Animation**: Bursts spores from its cap. Mini mushrooms sprout wherever they land (1 HP, weak melee)
- **Personality**: Amazed by its own abilities. *"Another one! Wait, am I doing that? That's so cool!"*
- **Purification Boss**: Poison Mushroom — a monster that zombified with toxic spores -> After purification, becomes a guardian that summons allied mini units

### Ch4: Marsh Series (Rotting Lake)

#### 13. Heron — Piercing Dealer [R1]
- **Appearance**: A heron with a long, sharp beak. Elegant posture
- **Why?**: Heron hunting method = precisely piercing through water with its long beak = the natural metaphor for piercing
- **Animation**: Pulls back its long neck then thrusts its beak. Pierces all enemies in its path. Elegantly raises its neck after striking
- **Personality**: Proud and patient. *"...I wait. And then pierce through in one strike."*

#### 14. Poison Frog — Debuffer [R10]
- **Appearance**: A colorful poison dart frog. Purple toxic fumes rise from its skin. Small but threatening colors
- **Why?**: Poison frog = poison. When it spews poison, enemies weaken — attack/defense reduction = debuff
- **Animation**: Puffs up its cheeks and blows purple poison mist at range. Enemies engulfed in the poison cloud show stat reduction icons above their heads. Poison cloud slowly spreads
- **Personality**: Small but confident. *"See my colors? That's a warning. Ignore it and it'll hurt~"*

#### 15. Turtle — Shield Tank [R20]
- **Appearance**: A turtle with a massive shell. Moss and flowers on the shell
- **Why?**: Shell = natural shield. Extends to cover nearby allies = barrier
- **Animation**: Expands a translucent green protective dome from shell. Shields allies in range. Regenerates even when broken
- **Personality**: Easygoing and relaxed. *"No rush. You're safe behind my shell."*

#### 16. Venus — %HP Damage Dealer [R40 Purification]
- **Appearance**: A carnivorous plant spirit with tooth-like leaves. Always has its mouth open
- **Why?**: Venus flytrap = devours anything. Same proportion regardless of size in one bite = %HP damage
- **Animation**: Opens tooth-leaves and chomps. Deals damage based on max HP. Chewing animation. Gets especially excited for big enemies
- **Personality**: Always hungry. *"So hungry~ A big one's coming! Looks delicious!"*
- **Purification Boss**: Giant Carnivorous Plant — a monster that devoured indiscriminately -> After purification, becomes a guardian that shaves down enemies' essence by percentage

### Ch5: Root Series (Arca's Roots)

#### 17. Frog — Isolation + Sustained Damage Dealer [R1]
- **Appearance**: A big, plump frog. Has a long, strong tongue. Cheeks puffed up large
- **Why?**: Frog = catching with tongue. Grabs enemy with long tongue, puts them in mouth and chews = isolation + sustained damage. Spits them out if they don't die. The frog's hunting method itself
- **Animation**: Shoots long tongue to grab enemy -> puts in mouth and chews (sustained damage) -> swallows on kill, spits out on timeout. Cheeks bulge while chewing
- **Personality**: Carefree and loves eating. *"Nom nom... ugh, this one tastes bad. Ptoo!"*

#### 18. Roottrap — Stun (Hard CC) Support [R10]
- **Appearance**: A mass of Arca's roots surging from underground
- **Why?**: Roots surge from the ground and grab enemies = can't move = stun
- **Animation**: Ground cracks and roots surge up. Wraps around enemy's ankles and binds. Short complete immobilization. Long cooldown
- **Personality**: Weighty ancient will. *"The earth holds you. Stop."*

#### 19. Crystal — Reflect Tank [R20]
- **Appearance**: A sparkling crystal formed from crystallized Arca sap
- **Why?**: When attacks hit the transparent crystal surface, they reflect as light = mirror reflection
- **Animation**: When hit by enemy attack, light reflects back to attacker. Rainbow prism effect
- **Personality**: Calm and transparent. *"I'll return your strength to you. Accept it gratefully."*

#### 20. Hawk — Execute Dealer [R40 Purification]
- **Appearance**: A raptor with glowing golden feathers. Sharp talons and keen eyes
- **Why?**: Hawk = hunter. Picks out weakened prey and finishes them decisively with a dive = nature's executioner
- **Animation**: Normally circles above. When enemy HP drops below a certain %, eyes flash and it dives! Powerful strike with golden talons. Golden feather explosion effect on successful execution. Returns to the sky after finishing
- **Personality**: Proud and precise. Says almost nothing but shows through action. *"...Spotted." (dives)*
- **Purification Boss**: Blighted Raptor — a monster that attacked anyone indiscriminately -> After purification, becomes the ultimate hunter who precisely strikes weak points to finish off the battlefield
- **Sprout's Dialogue**: *"You who used to swoop down on anyone... now you only fly in at the moment you're truly needed."*
