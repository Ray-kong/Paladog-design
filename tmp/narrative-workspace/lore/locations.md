# Location Document — v0.4 (Based on PD Finalized Version)

---

## [W-03] Last Garden — Player Base

- **Name**: Last Garden
- **Atmosphere**: Warm, vibrant, quiet sorrow
- **Description**: A small sanctuary where Arca's last healthy branch covers the sky. Soft light pours through the leaves, and pollen sparkles in the air. Beyond the borders, blackened, cracked earth stretches endlessly, a reminder of how fragile this beauty is.
- **Gameplay**: Home screen/base. Unit management, upgrades, stage selection.

---

## Ch1 Location: Garden Border — Sprout Series

- **Atmosphere**: Green, fresh, vigilant
- **Description**: The edge of the Last Garden. The last land touched by Arca's blessing. From here, the green gradually fades, and the aura of Blight slowly creeps in.
- **Environmental Effect**: None (tutorial)
- **Enemy Theme**: Blighted weeds, small insects — Blight's vanguard

---

## Ch2 Location: Forest Beyond the Garden — Forest Beast Series

- **Atmosphere**: Lush, wild, the boundary between blessing and Blight
- **Description**: Once the lushest forest in Edena. Blighted and healthy areas are mixed together like patches among the trees. Blessed animals are fighting against the Blight.
- **Environmental Effect**: Fog (limited visibility)
- **Enemy Theme**: Blighted forest animals — Frenzy Wolf, Blighted Boar

---

## Ch3 Location: Deep Ancient Forest — Ancient Tree Series

- **Atmosphere**: Majestic, serene, frozen in time
- **Description**: The deep forest where Arca's oldest branches extend. Trees hundreds of years old have been sleeping and are beginning to awaken with Sprout's arrival. The Blight has seeped even here, threatening these ancient beings.
- **Environmental Effect**: Poison fog (damage over time)
- **Enemy Theme**: Blighted ancient plants, twisted tree spirits

---

## [W-05] Ch4 Location: Rotting Lake — Marsh Series

- **Atmosphere**: Damp, toxic, tangled
- **Description**: Once the most fertile ecosystem in Edena, this great marsh has been transformed into a poison swamp by the Blight contaminating the water. Rotting aquatic plants are tangled together and poison fog drifts over the surface. However, some creatures have adapted to the toxins and evolved uniquely.
- **Environmental Effect**: Earthquake (periodic unit movement disruption)
- **Enemy Theme**: Blighted marsh creatures, poison aquatic plants, contaminated aquatic insects

---

## [W-06] Ch5 Location: Arca's Roots — Root Series

- **Atmosphere**: Primal, unstable, wondrous
- **Description**: The deepest part of the World Tree's roots. The place closest to the source of the Blight. Massive roots intertwine like caves, and crystallized sap glows on the walls. The boundary between Blight and life is most blurred here — Arca's most primal power exists in crystallized form.
- **Environmental Effect**: Blight storm (periodic damage to all units)
- **Enemy Theme**: Entities that are the Blight itself given form, Blight cores
