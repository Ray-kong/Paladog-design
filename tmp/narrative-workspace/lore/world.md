## 1. World Background — Core Conflict

### World: Edena

A continent where all life once lived in harmony under the blessing of **the great World Tree "Arca"**.
Arca's roots span the entire continent, and its sap is life force itself — from the smallest flower to sentient races, all were born from Arca's energy.

**"The Withering"** — One day, an unexplained **Blight** surged from deep within Arca's roots. Living beings touched by the Blight lose their reason and go berserk, the earth cracks and blackens, and forests twist. The Blight spread rapidly through the roots, consuming 90% of the continent.

The survivors gathered beneath Arca's last healthy branch. This is the **"Last Garden"** — the only sanctuary untouched by the Blight.

### Why Defense?
Blighted beings ceaselessly surge toward the Last Garden. This is because Arca's blessing draws them in. The player's ultimate goal is to defend this garden while gradually purifying the blighted land and reclaiming territory.

### Core Questions (Lore Hooks)
- What is the true cause of the Blight? (Main story arc)
- Why can't Arca heal itself?
- Has something like this happened before? (Ancient history lore)

## 3. Protagonist — Sprout

Arca's last sprout. During the Withering, Arca produced several sprouts, but all others were consumed by the Blight — only Sprout survived. Arca is already too weakened to produce new sprouts.

**Sprout's death = defeat.** If Sprout falls, the connection with Arca is severed, there is no one left to summon Guardians, and the Last Garden's barrier collapses.

See `characters.md` for detailed profile.

## 4. Chapter World Settings

| Chapter | Series | Location | Narrative |
|---------|--------|----------|-----------|
| Ch1 | Sprout | Around the Last Garden | First battles at the garden border. Arca's youngest spirits awaken |
| Ch2 | Forest | Forest beyond the garden | Venturing outside the garden for the first time. Forest animals awaken upon receiving the blessing |
| Ch3 | Ancient | Deep ancient forest | Where Arca's ancient branches, dormant for centuries, begin to reawaken |
| Ch4 | Marsh | Rotting Lake | A wetland deeply permeated by the Blight. Creatures that adapted to the toxins evolved uniquely |
| Ch5 | Root | Arca's Roots | The deepest part of the World Tree's roots. The place closest to the source of the Blight |

**Story Progression**: Garden -> Forest -> Deep Forest -> Blight Border (Marsh) -> Source of the Blight (Roots).
